Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Common questions asked by vulnerable groups, including elderly individuals, migrants, and others with linguistic challenges.

For Elderly Individuals

  • Q: How can I lodge my tax return if I don't have internet access?
    A: You can lodge a paper tax return by mail, or visit a local tax agent for assistance.
  • Q: Are there any special tax benefits for pensioners?
    A: Yes, certain concessions and rebates are available for senior citizens and pensioners.

For Migrants and People with Linguistic Challenges

  • Q: Can I get tax assistance in my native language?
    A: Yes, the ATO provides interpreters for a variety of languages through the Translating and Interpreting Service (TIS).

General Tax Questions

  • Q: What documents do I need to file my tax return?
    A: You will need your tax file number (TFN), income statements, and receipts for any deductions.
  • Q: How long do I have to keep my tax records?
    A: You should keep your tax records for at least five years in case of an audit.
